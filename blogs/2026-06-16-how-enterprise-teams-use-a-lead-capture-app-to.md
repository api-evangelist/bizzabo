A modern event lead capture app does more than scan badges; it qualifies leads, captures contextual notes, scores prospects, and syncs data to CRM in real time, helping enterprise teams convert event interactions into pipeline contributions rather than flat contact lists. The piece cites Bizzabo CEO Eran Ben-Shushan that events are now positioned as a core growth system evaluated on pipeline creation, deal acceleration, and customer retention.
